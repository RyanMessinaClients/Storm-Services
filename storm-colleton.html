Founder & Lead Agent — Storm Colleton & Associates
Storm Colleton is a highly decorated executive protection specialist with over 20 years of experience safeguarding some of the world's most high-profile individuals, organizations, and faith communities.
A former New Jersey State Police officer and trained security operative, Storm founded Storm Colleton & Associates with a singular mission: to provide world-class protection that is as effective as it is precise. His approach combines military precision with extensive field experience — ensuring every client receives protection tailored to their specific environment and needs.
Storm has led protection operations across the United States and internationally, covering heads of industry, faith leaders, celebrities, and dignitaries. His reputation for professionalism, discretion, and mission success has made him one of the most trusted names in executive protection.
